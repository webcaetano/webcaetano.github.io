Tags

Phaser

Wizard of the shrine is my first Phaser game.
It was made at December 2015.

The challenge part was the art.
Drawing and panting is not my thing.
I had to study a lot to improve my illustration skills
Here a few 'timelapse' from the artwork :



The whole game development time-wise was 3 months.
The majority of time was spent doing the assets.

Things that i learn from this project

  • This was my first game from Phaser. It is a lot better than Flash. But Phaser does not have all Flash features.
  • We live in a time that we have Steam, Apple Store, Play Store, Amazon Store.
    Making a game that does not fit for any of those marketplaces is a waste of time.
    And Phaser fit them all. But you have to keep this in mind at the beginning of the development, not at the end.
