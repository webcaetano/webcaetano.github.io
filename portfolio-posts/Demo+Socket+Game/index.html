Multiplayer isometric plataform, with tilesets.
Monsters movements are operated by a nodejs server.
I had to make a map-editor for this plataform, you can see it here.

This project is a MMORPG isometric, based on Ragnarok Online
On this demo, the orcs are players and the pink blobs are cpu-monsters

I have switch this whole project to Phaser but its not live.

  • Click black bar, type, and hit enter to chat
